## üöÄ Our Mission

Our mission is to create a high-level, open-source, and experimental framework for stylized non-human ethical vocal synthesis. 

The **[Alizarin Engine](https://github.com/ALIZARINENGINE/AlizarinEngine)** is a project designed to generate multiple types of voice products‚Äîincluding real-time TTS, AI singing, and concatenative singing‚Äîall from a single, 100% synthetic voice source. We are focused on empowering creators with powerful tools to author entirely new synthetic voices, giving them deep control to produce heavily stylized and unique timbres, such as those inspired by retro speech synthesis.

## üíñ Character Rights & Special Requests

This section covers the usage rights for our official characters (like the Alizarin Engine Voice) and their associated IP. While our engine is open-source, our official characters are our brand, and their use is more strictly controlled.

**Non-commercial artistic fan works are encouraged!** For all other uses, please see the **"Special Requests" process (detailed in our `USAGE_GUIDE.md`)** if you wish to:

* Request permission to use an official character's name for song credits (e.g., "Song featuring ALIZARIN").
* Inquire about a commercial license for using the character's name or artwork (e.g., on merchandise or album covers).
* Propose an official collaboration or addition to one of our main, official voicebanks.
* Request a one time exception for a use case restricted by our standard character rights (like R-18 content).
